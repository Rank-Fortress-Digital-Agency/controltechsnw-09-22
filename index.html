Ductless Mini-Split Heat Pump Service


Ductless mini-split heat pumps are a great way to heat and cool your home without requiring ductwork. They work by using a series of fans to move air around your home, which then circulates through the mini-split heat pump to either heat or cool the air. This makes them an excellent option for homes without existing ductwork or having their ductwork damaged somehow. Mini-split heat pumps can also be an excellent option for additions or new construction since they can be installed quickly and don’t require significant modifications to your home.

If you’re thinking about getting a ductless mini-split heat pump for your home, it’s essential to make sure that you find a reputable and experienced contractor to install it. This type of system is very different from a traditional forced air system, so the installation must be done correctly to avoid any problems.

Once your ductless mini-split heat pump is installed, it must be serviced regularly to keep it running correctly.
